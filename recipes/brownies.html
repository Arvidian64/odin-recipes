Brownies

These vegan brownies are made with cocoa powder for a rich and gooey chocolaty treat.

If you prefer brownies that are a little more solid,

you can bake the brownies for longer than the recommended time

Ingredients

  • 6 deciliters flour
  • 6 deciliters granulated sugar
  • 2 deciliters unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 0.75 teaspoon salt
  • 3 deciliters hot water or coffee
  • 1.5 deciliters vegetable oil
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 175 degrees celsius
  2. Grease a baking dish, lin bottom with parchment paper
  3. Whisk flour, sugar cocoa powder, baking powder, and salt together.
  4. pour in water/coffee, vegetable oil, and vanilla; mix then spread batter evenly in the prepared dish
  5. bake in oven until top is no longer shiny and center feels set, about 30 minutes
  6. Let cool for at least 10 minutes before cutting into squares.
  7. Enjoy!
