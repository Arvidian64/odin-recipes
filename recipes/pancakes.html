Pancakes

Fluffy vegan pancakes!

Ingredients

  • 3.5 deciliters flour
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 0.5 teaspoons salt
  • 3.5 deciliters water
  • 1 tablespoon oil

Steps

  1. Sift flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt into a large bowl
  2. Whisk together water and oil in a small bowl
  3. Pour oil and water mixture into flour mixture
  4. Stir until blended; mixture will be lumpy
  5. Heat a lightly oiled griddle, medium-high heat
  6. Drop batter by spoonfulls onto griddle.
  7. Cook until bubbles form and edges are dry
  8. Flip, then cook until bottoms are browned, 1 to 2 minutes
  9. Repeat with remaining batter
  10. Serve with berries
